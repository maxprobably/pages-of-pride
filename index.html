Pages
of
Pride

The LGBT Center of Raleigh is proud to announce Pages of Pride, a family friendly literary festival celebrating all things queer literature!

In the face of news about LGBTQ+ centred books being challenged and censored, the Center is providing the Triangle community an opportunity to engage with books, honor the stories of LGBTQ+ people, and celebrate.

Location:

Apex Nature Park - Amphitheatre and Large Shelter
2600 Evans Road
Apex, NC
27502

11 AM - 3 PM
